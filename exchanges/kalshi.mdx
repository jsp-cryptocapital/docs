# Kalshi

## Overview

US CFTC-regulated Designated Contract Market (DCM) deploying domestic event contracts. Operates in hard cash strings (\_dollars) on its updated V2 API architecture, utilizing its in-house clearinghouse (Kalshi Klear).

## Fee Structure

**Maker Fee:** Charged dynamically on executed resting limit orders depending on contract type.

**Taker Fee:** Enforces a formulaic bell-shaped percentage of expected contract earnings:

$\text{Fee} = \lceil 0.07 \times \text{Contracts} \times P \times (1 - P) \rceil$

Fees peak at $50\%$ probability — $P = 0.50$ costs $\$1.75\$ per 100 contracts — and trend to zero at tail probabilities.

## Active Execution Strategies

- Real-time political structural arbitrage matching decentralized offshore indices
- High-frequency macro economic data release market-making (BLS, NFP, CPI tracking)
